About Us

Our Story

We always hear stories about people's first dogs or cats. Have you heard stories about people's first snakes? We at ShelterYou provide shelter for to all types of snakes and horses. Our aim is to make sure every one of these animals we come across finds a place to call home. A pet can change a person's life, so why not do the same for them.


Our creators Harsh Aggrawal, Jamshed Jahangir and Tausif Zaman have spent a lot of their lives for working for the betterment of animals. In this chapter of their lives they are planning to help out as many animals as possible. This website has helped with the successful relocation of 50+ animals already.
